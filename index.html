Axiom

Learn real algebra by proving theorems yourself.

About

Axiom is a mathematics game where you start from the foundational axioms of algebra and build your way up by proving real theorems, step by step. Every rule you use was either given as an axiom or proven by you in a previous level.

Privacy Policy

Axiom does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts required. Your progress is stored locally on your device only.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Drag tiles and apply algebraic rules to transform equations. Each level presents a theorem to prove using only the axioms and theorems you've already established.

Is this app free?

Axiom is free to download and play.

Does it work offline?

Yes — Axiom works entirely offline. No internet connection required.
